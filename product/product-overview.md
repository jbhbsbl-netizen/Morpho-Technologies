# Morpho Technologies

## One-Line Pitch
"We customize attritable, shallow-water, composite-based AUVs at 10-100x lower cost than existing systems."

## The Problem
Industry AUVs cost $50k-$500k+, making them too expensive to risk in contested environments, swarm operations, or high-risk reconnaissance missions. Defense and maritime operators need cost-effective platforms that can be deployed in scenarios where loss is acceptable.

## The Proof
We built a functional composite-based AUV for ~$3500 — demonstrating radical cost reduction is possible.

## The Opportunity
At this price point, mission profiles that require attritable platforms become economically viable:
- Swarm operations
- High-risk reconnaissance in contested waters
- Shallow-water/littoral zone operations
- Mine countermeasures
- Expendable ISR (Intelligence, Surveillance, Reconnaissance)

## Success Looks Like
A professional, credible website that:
- Establishes legitimacy for funding applications
- Shows we've built something real (proof of concept)
- Highlights cost advantage without revealing proprietary details
